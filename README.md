# E-Commerce Sales & Customer Analytics

## Overview

This project analyzes the Olist Brazilian E-Commerce dataset to uncover sales performance, customer behavior, revenue trends, and retention opportunities. The analysis combines Python for data cleaning and exploratory analysis, MySQL for data storage and querying, and Tableau for interactive business dashboards.

The goal is to transform raw transactional data into actionable business insights that support strategic decision-making.

---

## Business Objectives

* Analyze overall sales performance and revenue trends.
* Understand customer purchasing behavior.
* Identify high-value and at-risk customer segments using RFM Analysis.
* Evaluate payment preferences and order fulfillment performance.
* Build executive-level dashboards for business stakeholders.

## Key Insights

### Sales Performance

* Revenue exceeded **$16M** during the analysis period.
* Monthly revenue showed strong growth throughout 2017 and early 2018.
* Revenue peaked at approximately **$1.2M** in a single month.

### Customer Analytics

* RFM analysis identified **Potential Customers** as the largest segment.
* A significant percentage of customers made only one purchase, indicating opportunities for retention campaigns.
* Customer segmentation can help target loyalty and re-engagement strategies.

### Payment Behavior

* Credit cards accounted for the majority of transactions.
* Alternative payment methods represented a relatively small share of total purchases.

### Order Fulfillment

* Most orders were successfully delivered.
* Delivery performance metrics highlighted opportunities to improve customer experience.
